How to prepare for a Roof Inspection

  • ✓Roof Inspections take between 30-45 min.
  • ✓ Please make sure the residence is accessible and pets are secured.
  • ✓ Nobody needs to be home for the roof inspection. We just need access to the roof.
  • ✓ These roof inspections are done hands-on from the top of the roof by one of our expert inspectors.
  • ✓Roof inspections are completed within 1 business day of booking most of the time. Always completed within 2 business days.

What is included in the Inspection Report?

  • ✓ We start by walking the roof surface visually inspecting for any signs of leaks, wear and tear, damage, vegeation overgrowth, mistakes from a previous roofer.
  • ✓ We also look for signs of the roof age and its lifespan.
  • ✓We check to see if drains and gutters are visually not clogged.
  • ✓We check all flashings and around the chimney area for any issues.
  • ✓ We check for structural damage such as a sagging roof. We look for missing tile shingle peices.
  • ✓ We take note and document any issues found in any of these areas on the roof.
  • ✓ We take pictures of everything we are inspecting and include them in our report.
  • ✓ Once the roof inspection is complete, a verbal report will be given in person or over the phone and you will receive an email with your detailed report and images.
  • ✓ We will give guidance on the next best steps for any issues we find.
