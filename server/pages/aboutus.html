About Us

Rick & Justin Kludy

Ceo & Owner

San Diego Roof Inspections is a local San Diego firm, father and son roof inspection team. For over 45 years SDRI has been involved in many aspects of the roofing industry. Starting with learning to fix small repairs from grandfather, than progressing into 25 years of hands-on roofing experience.

-Roofing in 1991-

This was followed by 15 years dedicated to roof litigation and 5 years of conducting roof inspections in collaboration with realtors. Over the last two decades, we have been passing this knowledge on to the next generation, providing our team with the necessary experience to conduct our roofing services. At this point its more than just a career, its a lifestyle.


Our expertise has allowed us to see a failure in the marketplace, which is the conflict of intertest contractors offer with their roof inspection service and their contracted roofing services. We realized unbiased roof inspections is where we fit into the equation.


After seeing so many homeowners pay for unecessary reroofs throughout the years we have realized our values align the most with giving honest evaluations and pointing people in the right direction.


San Diego Roof Inspectors are experts in the field of roof repairs with years of experience dissecting roofs and finding causes of leaks all over southern California.

-Making a roofing repair in 2023-
